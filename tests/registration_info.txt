
# Q: How can I register for CM Cup 2025?
A: You can register for the Chief Minister's Cup (CM Cup) 2025 by visiting the official website at **cmcup.telangana.gov.in**. 
Click on the "Register" button on the homepage. You will need to provide your basic details such as Name, Age, Gender, Mobile Number, and select your District, Mandal, and Village.
After submitting the form, you will receive a registration ID on your registered mobile number.

# Q: Who is eligible to participate in the CM Cup?
A: The competition is open to all sports enthusiasts in Telangana. There are different age categories: 
1. **Under-15 (Boys & Girls)**
2. **Under-17 (Boys & Girls)**
3. **Under-19 (Boys & Girls)**
4. **General Category (Men & Women - 15 to 36 years)**

# Q: What documents are required for registration?
A: You will need a valid ID proof (Aadhaar Card, Student ID, or Voter ID) to verify your age and residency during the event. For online registration, you just need your mobile number and basic details.

# Q: Can I register for multiple sports?
A: Yes, a player can register for multiple sports disciplines, provided the schedules do not clash. However, you must register separately for each discipline if required by the portal.

# Q: Is there a registration fee?
A: No, registration for the CM Cup is completely **free of charge**.

# Q: How do I know my team or match schedule?
A: After registration, the fixture and schedule details will be communicated via SMS and will also be available on the official website **cmcup.telangana.gov.in** under the "Schedules" section.
