SOURCE CONTEXT
This document contains IT Team and technical support assignments for the Chief Minister’s Cup 2025. It maps Special Officers, districts, and IT / helpdesk personnel responsible for technology operations, registrations, training, and system support.

PURPOSE
The IT Team supports CM Cup 2025 operations across districts by handling:
- Technical coordination
- Registration systems
- Helpdesk support

STRUCTURE OVERVIEW

- Assigned IT / Technical team members
