EVENT: Chief Minister’s Cup 2025 – Special Officers & Monitoring Structure

SOURCE CONTEXT
This document is an official proceeding issued by the Vice Chairman & Managing Director,
Sports Authority of Telangana (SATG), Lal Bahadur Stadium, Hyderabad.
It relates to the nomination of Special Officers and Assistants for the 2nd Edition of the
Chief Minister’s Cup 2025, conducted from village level to state level across 33 districts.

ORDER DETAILS
Proceedings Number: SATG/T&S/CM’s Cup 25/696/2025
Order Date: 27 December 2025
Reference: G.O. Rt. No. 391, dated 18.12.2025, Youth Advancement & Culture (Sports) Department, Government of Telangana.

PURPOSE OF NOMINATION
The Government of Telangana accorded permission to conduct the 2nd Edition of the
Chief Minister’s Cup 2025 with the vision:
“Rural to Global – Crafting Champions”.

The Sports Authority of Telangana (SATG) nominated Special Officers and Assistants
for all 33 districts to handle:
- Overall in-charge responsibilities
- Registration
- Monitoring
- Smooth conduct of competitions
- Torch Relay coordination

EVENT DURATION
Chief Minister’s Cup 2025:
- From: 05 January 2026
- To: 24 February 2026
(Including CM’s Cup Torch Relay)

ROLE DEFINITIONS
Special Officer:
- Overall in-charge at district level
- Responsible for coordination, supervision, and execution
- Acts as nodal authority for the district

Assistant Officers / Coaches:
- Support Special Officer
- Handle discipline-specific coordination
- Assist in trials, registrations, and monitoring

DISTRICT-WISE STRUCTURE
Each district in Telangana is assigned:
- One Special Officer
- Multiple Assistant Officers / Coaches
- One District Youth Sports Officer (DYSO) for coordination

AUTHORITY & COMMUNICATION
- Officers function under the administrative control of SATG.
- They are responsible for guiding all concerned stakeholders
  for successful conduct of CM Cup 2025 at village, mandal,
  constituency, district, and state levels.

ISSUING AUTHORITY
Vice Chairman & Managing Director,
Sports Authority of Telangana
