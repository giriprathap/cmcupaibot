SOURCE CONTEXT
This document is derived from the official Excel file titled “MANDAL LEVEL CLUSTER INCHARGE NAME & PH.NO.xlsx”. It lists mandal-level cluster in-charges appointed for the Chief Minister’s Cup 2025 to manage coordination and execution.

PURPOSE
The mandal-level cluster in-charge framework supports:
- Local coordination of CM Cup activities
- Oversight of cluster-wise execution
- Liaison between Gram Panchayat, Mandal, and District authorities

STRUCTURE OVERVIEW
Each mandal or cluster has:
- One designated Cluster In-Charge
- Responsibility for trials, registrations, and ground-level coordination
