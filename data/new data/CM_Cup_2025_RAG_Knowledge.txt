EVENT: Chief Minister’s Cup 2025 (2nd Edition)

OVERVIEW
The 2nd Edition of the Chief Minister’s Cup 2025 is a state-level multi-sport tournament organized to promote sports development from rural to global levels. The event emphasizes mass participation, structured competition, and alignment with National Sports Federation guidelines.

TORCH RALLY (AWARENESS PROGRAM)
Start Date: 08 January 2026
End Date: 17 January 2026
Duration: 10 days
Venues:
- Tennis Complex, LBS (Option-1)
- District Headquarters (Option-2)

TOURNAMENT SCHEDULE BY LEVEL
Grampanchayat Level:
- 17 January to 22 January 2026 (6 days)

Mandal / Urban Local Body (ULB) Level:
- 28 January to 31 January 2026 (4 days)

Assembly Constituency Level:
- 03 February to 07 February 2026 (5 days)

District Level:
- 10 February to 14 February 2026 (5 days)

State Level Competitions:
- 19 February to 26 February 2026 (8 days)
- Conducted at multiple venues across the state

PLAYER CATEGORIES & AGE GROUPS
Competitive Games:
- Sub-Junior
- Junior
- Senior

Recreational Games:
- Children
- Others

Para Games:
- Open Category

ADMINISTRATIVE STRUCTURE
- Each Urban Local Body (ULB) is treated as one unit.
- Each Municipal Corporation Zone is treated as one unit, except GHMC.
- District Collectors are authorized to finalize the number of games and disciplines at GP, Mandal, ULB, Assembly Constituency, and District levels.

APPROVED SPORTS DISCIPLINES
Athletics, Archery, Badminton, Boxing, Swimming, Wrestling, Table Tennis, Lawn Tennis, Weightlifting, Gymnastics, Shooting, Basketball, Handball, Hockey, Football, Volleyball, Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, Sepak Takraw, Chess, Baseball, Netball, Kickboxing, Cycling, Rowing, Squash Racket, Canoeing & Kayaking, Wushu, Atya-Patya, Powerlifting, Softball, Taekwondo, Billiards & Snookers, Judo, Karate, Yoga, Skating, Fencing, Pickle Ball, Sailing, Ball Badminton, Mallakhamb, Para Games, Recreational Games (Children/Others).

CHAMPIONSHIP POINTS SYSTEM

Individual Sports (Athletics, Badminton, Karate, Yoga, Chess):
- 1st Position: 5 points
- 2nd Position: 3 points
- 3rd Position: 2 points

Team Sports (Basketball, Handball, Hockey, Football, Volleyball, Kabaddi, Kho-Kho):
- 1st Position: 10 points
- 2nd Position: 6 points
- 3rd Position: 4 points

GOVERNING COMMITTEE
- Telangana Olympic Association
- Concerned Sports Association President / General Secretary
- Concerned In-charge Coaches
