EVENT: Chief Minister’s Cup 2025 – Cluster Mapping (Vikarabad District)

SOURCE CONTEXT
This document contains the official cluster-wise mapping of Gram Panchayats (GPs) and Police Stations (PSs) for the conduct of CM Cup 2025 in Vikarabad district. It is used for planning, coordination, trials, and administrative execution.

PURPOSE
The cluster mapping is intended to:
- Organize Gram Panchayats into manageable clusters
- Align Police Station jurisdictions with clusters
- Support smooth execution of CM Cup activities at village and mandal levels

CLUSTER STRUCTURE OVERVIEW
Each cluster generally includes:
- One Cluster identifier or name
- Multiple Gram Panchayats (GPs)
- One or more Police Stations (PSs) for jurisdiction and coordination
